1. Services

Tiny Hearts 0130 provides mobile childcare services throughout Costa Rica. Our caregivers are certified in CPR and First Aid. Services are provided at the client's location (hotel, vacation rental, home, or event venue) anywhere in Costa Rica.

2. Booking and Confirmation

All bookings are subject to availability. A booking is confirmed only after we have acknowledged receipt of your request and confirmed the date and time. We reserve the right to decline any booking at our discretion.

3. Pricing and Payment

  • Rates are $15/hour for 1 child, $25/hour for 2 children, and $35/hour for 3 children.
  • A minimum of 4 hours applies to all bookings.
  • Holiday rates: $5/hour surcharge applies on Costa Rican public holidays.
  • A 30% security deposit is required to confirm your booking.
  • Payment methods accepted: Cash (local currency) and PayPal.
  • An electronic receipt will be provided after payment.

4. Cancellation Policy

Cancellations must be made at least 72 hours before the scheduled service time to receive a full refund of the security deposit. Cancellations made less than 72 hours before the service may result in forfeiture of the deposit, at our discretion.

5. Client Responsibilities

  • Provide accurate information about the number and ages of children.
  • Disclose any allergies, medical conditions, or special needs before the service begins.
  • Ensure a safe and suitable environment for childcare at the designated location.
  • Be present or have a designated responsible adult available for the duration of the service.
